I'm a Researcher at Facebook AI.

I received PhD from University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, and my advisor was Prof. Tamara Berg. Before that, I got Sc.M from Brown University, working with Prof. James Hays, and B.Eng. in Software Engineering from Tongji University.

My research interests span Computer Vision, especially video and audio cross-modeling; Machine Learning and Computational Photography from time to time.
